GOOD LISTENER
    1. Give the speaker your undivided attention
    2. Providing a speaker with the appropriate nonverbal cues can help them feel more at ease and comfortable as you listen to them.
    3. Being a good listener often includes opening a dialogue and allowing for a conversation to start between you and the speaker.
    4. Being a good listener encompasses both processing a speaker's message and providing valuable input from your point of view.
